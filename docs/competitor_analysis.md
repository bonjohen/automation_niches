# COI Tracking Competitor Analysis

## Market Overview

The Certificate of Insurance (COI) tracking market is well-established with several mature players. Key trends in 2025:

- **AI Evolution**: Moving from basic OCR to autonomous "agents" that interpret policies, flag gaps, and execute workflows
- **Pricing**: $6-$80 per vendor per year based on automation depth
- **Pain Point**: Organizations spend 15-20 hours weekly on manual certificate tracking
- **Compliance Gap**: Even 95% accuracy leaves businesses vulnerable; leading platforms achieve 90%+ compliance vs 60-70% industry average

---

## Major Competitors

### 1. myCOI
- **Founded**: 2009 (established player)
- **Recent News**: Launched "illumend" AI-powered experience in May 2025
- **Industries**: Construction, Real Estate, Property Management, Manufacturing, Retail, Transportation, Utilities, Government
- **Positioning**: Enterprise-focused, comprehensive compliance auditing
- **URL**: https://mycoitracking.com

### 2. bcs (Business Credentialing Services)
- **Key Feature**: "RiskBot" - first autonomous COI tracking agent (March 2025)
- **Differentiator**: Free tier for up to 25 vendors
- **AI Capability**: Autonomous agent that interprets policies, flags gaps, executes workflows
- **URL**: https://www.getbcs.com

### 3. Jones (GetJones)
- **Funding**: $15M Series B (January 2025) for AI agent development
- **Focus**: Property management and construction
- **Integrations**: Procore, MRI Software
- **Target**: Large-scale operations
- **URL**: https://getjones.com

### 4. TrustLayer
- **Focus**: AI-powered automated collection and verification
- **Features**: Real-time insurance checks
- **Integrations**: Construction software (Procore)
- **Target**: Construction industry

### 5. CertFocus (Vertikal RMS)
- **Key Technology**: "Hawk-I AI" for certificate review
- **Claim**: Customers jump from <60% to >90% compliance
- **Positioning**: Most accurate certificate tracking
- **URL**: https://www.vertikalrms.com

### 6. SmartCompliance
- **Positioning**: Honest about capabilities (OCR + automation, not AI agents)
- **Target**: Organizations needing basic tracking with modest expectations
- **Differentiator**: Straightforward, functional software without hype

### 7. Certificial
- **Key Technology**: "Smart COI" - real-time policy monitoring
- **Unique Feature**: Captures mid-term cancellations within seconds of agent system changes
- **Claim**: 90%+ compliance rates vs 60-70% industry average
- **URL**: https://www.certificial.com

### 8. SimpleCerts
- **Model**: AI + human verification hybrid
- **Target**: Property managers, contractors, risk professionals
- **Positioning**: Affordable, fully managed service
- **URL**: https://simplecerts.ai

## Key Insights for Development

1. **AI is table stakes**: Basic OCR/extraction is expected; differentiate on UX and SMB focus
2. **Free tier works**: bcs offers free for 25 vendors - consider freemium model
3. **Integration matters**: Procore, MRI Software integrations are common - prioritize CRM over construction software for SMB
4. **Real-time monitoring is premium**: Certificial's real-time policy monitoring is unique - not needed for MVP
5. **Human verification hybrid**: SimpleCerts combines AI + human review - consider for high-value documents
