Core Platform Features

Built for small businesses that need enterprise-level compliance without the complexity.

AI-Powered Document Processing

Upload any compliance document and let our AI do the heavy lifting. Using advanced OCR and large language models, we extract key data points automatically:

  • Policy numbers and coverage limits
  • Expiration and effective dates
  • Named insureds and additional parties
  • Endorsements and exclusions
  • Contract terms and obligations

Smart Alert System

Never let a deadline slip through the cracks. Our multi-stage notification system keeps you ahead of every expiration:

  • 60, 30, 14, 7, and 3 day advance warnings
  • Customizable escalation workflows
  • Email, SMS, and in-app notifications
  • Automatic vendor/contractor reminders
  • Overdue alerts with management escalation

Real-Time Compliance Dashboard

Get instant visibility into your entire compliance status from a single unified dashboard:

  • Traffic-light status indicators (green/yellow/red)
  • Upcoming expirations calendar view
  • Vendor/entity compliance scorecards
  • Trend analysis and compliance history
  • Export reports for audits and reviews

Automated Workflow Engine

Set it and forget it. Our workflow automation handles repetitive tasks so your team can focus on what matters:

  • Auto-create requirements when vendors added
  • Link uploaded documents to entities automatically
  • Trigger renewal requests before expiration
  • Update compliance status based on document data
  • Route tasks to appropriate team members

YAML-Configurable for Any Industry

Our platform adapts to your specific compliance needs through flexible configuration:

  • 15+ pre-built industry templates
  • Custom entity types and fields
  • Configurable document extraction rules
  • Industry-specific notification templates
  • Tailored compliance checklists

CRM Integration Ready

Connect to your existing systems and keep everything in sync:

  • HubSpot and Salesforce connectors
  • Zapier integration for any app
  • Two-way contact/vendor sync
  • Push compliance status to CRM
  • Webhook support for custom integrations
