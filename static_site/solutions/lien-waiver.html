Lien Waiver & Pay Application Automation

Automate the collection and verification of lien waivers from subcontractors. Accelerate payment cycles and eliminate the compliance bottleneck that delays projects.

Pain Points

  • Payment bottleneck: The lien waiver process is highly manual and delays payments for entire projects - payments can't be released until all waivers are in
  • Paperwork overload: Waivers are drafted on paper or PDFs, emailed around, printed, scanned - a process rife with error and loss
  • Errors and compliance risks: Using outdated or incorrect state-specific waiver forms can invalidate the waiver and cause legal exposure
  • Missed waivers and disputes: Subcontractors sometimes forget or resist returning waivers, risking surprise liens despite payment. Industry loses nearly $177B annually to this
  • High volume and fragmentation: Each monthly pay application might involve dozens of waivers with each sub-tier contractor using their own tracking method

Typical Workflow Today

  • At billing cycle end, accountants compile subcontractor pay applications and draft lien waiver forms for each sub - often modifying last month's form
  • Waiver forms are emailed to each subcontractor for signature, who prints, signs, scans, and emails back (or uses DocuSign)
  • Returned waivers are saved as PDFs and checked off a spreadsheet - someone manually verifies each waiver is correct and complete
  • Only once all required waivers are in hand can the general contractor approve the payment application and release checks
  • Larger contractors use solutions like Procore or Textura for pay applications, but many SMBs still handle waivers via email/PDF

Why It's Hard

  • Multitude of documents: Each month, every active subcontractor needs potentially two waivers (conditional and unconditional). For 20 subs, that's 40+ documents monthly
  • Coordination stress: This process involves many external parties with limited control - one forgetful sub can hold up the entire chain
  • Human error and legal nuance: Using the wrong state-specific waiver form or missing a required field can nullify the waiver entirely
  • Time pressure: Everyone wants to get paid quickly, but the waiver process creates a rushed environment prone to mistakes
  • Lack of visibility: In a manual process, it's difficult to know in real time which waivers are outstanding and which payments are held up

How Automation Helps

  • Auto-generating correct forms: Platform determines the proper waiver type for each scenario and auto-fills with project and payment details, ensuring statutory compliance
  • Digital distribution and e-signatures: Route waivers to subcontractors via a portal or secure link for electronic signature, speeding up turnaround
  • Real-time tracking and reminders: Dashboard shows all waivers for a pay period with automatic reminders to any sub who hasn't signed
  • Integration with pay applications: Tie lien waivers directly to payment applications - a pay app cannot move to "approved" until all required waivers are uploaded
  • Centralized document repository: All signed waivers stored in a searchable cloud database organized by project and pay period
  • Error reduction and compliance safeguards: System validates inputs and uses the latest required legal wording for each state
  • Accelerated payments and cash flow: Automated processing speeds up the entire payment cycle - what took days or weeks can happen in minutes
  • Audit trail and visibility: Every action is logged - when a waiver was generated, sent, viewed, and signed - providing proof if disputes arise
