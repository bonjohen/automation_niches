Fleet Compliance Document Management

Automate DOT compliance tracking for vehicle registrations, driver certifications, CDLs, medical certificates, and inspection records. Never miss a renewal deadline again.

Pain Points

  • Scattered documentation: Records spread across spreadsheets, email chains, file cabinets, and even trucks, making it hard to stay organized
  • High regulatory complexity: Fleets must maintain driver lists, CDLs, medical certificates, vehicle inspection reports, insurance proofs, and drug testing records, each with specific retention requirements
  • Risk of missing critical deadlines: Manual tracking often fails, leading to lapsed registrations, missed MCS-150 updates, or expired certifications
  • Human error and volume pressure: Entering data by hand introduces mistakes that propagate through multiple documents, increasing legal and safety risks

Typical Workflow Today

  • Safety or compliance managers use spreadsheets and calendars to log expiration dates and requirements
  • Physical paperwork stored in binders or local drives, relying on email to chase documents from drivers
  • Key systems include FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse and state DMV websites, but these are siloed with no integration
  • Some fleets use telematics platforms like Samsara or KeepTruckin, but compliance info lives in disconnected places

Why It's Hard

  • Fragmentation: Each compliance area might be tracked in a different place by different people, causing items to fall through the cracks
  • Volume and frequency: A fleet of 20-50 vehicles generates hundreds of compliance documents annually
  • Human error: Manual data entry and reminder systems are prone to mistakes that stay hidden until audits
  • High stakes: Non-compliance can trigger fines, out-of-service orders, or liability in accidents
  • Geographically dispersed operations: Multi-site fleets face inconsistent processes at different locations

How Automation Helps

  • Centralized digital repository: Create a single source of truth for all documents with cloud storage and tags for drivers/units
  • Automated tracking & alerts: System monitors renewal deadlines and sends custom reminders before due dates
  • Workflow automation: Templated processes for onboarding new drivers, with auto-generated qualification files
  • OCR and data entry reduction: Ingest documents via OCR, extract key data, and validate entries automatically
  • Real-time compliance dashboards: See which drivers or vehicles are compliant and which have pending issues at a glance
  • Legal and audit readiness: Maintain proper retention of records with an audit trail demonstrating compliance
