AI Document Processing

Upload documents and let AI extract key data automatically. Certificates, contracts, and compliance documents processed in seconds with industry-leading accuracy.

How It Works

  • Upload any format: Drop PDFs, scanned images, photos, or email attachments directly into the platform - no pre-processing required
  • OCR + AI extraction: Our pipeline combines optical character recognition with large language models to understand document structure and extract key fields
  • Structured data output: Extracted information is automatically mapped to the appropriate fields - dates, amounts, policy numbers, coverage limits, and more
  • Confidence scoring: Each extracted field includes a confidence score so you know when to review manually vs. trust automation
  • Human-in-the-loop: Review and edit extracted data before it's committed - the system learns from your corrections over time

Insurance & COI Tracking

For property managers, general contractors, and procurement teams managing vendor relationships:

  • Certificate of Insurance (COI): Automatically extract insurer name, policy number, coverage types (GL, Auto, Umbrella, Workers' Comp), coverage limits, effective dates, and expiration dates
  • Additional Insured endorsements: AI identifies whether your company is listed as additional insured and flags missing endorsements
  • Coverage gap detection: Compares extracted limits against your minimum requirements and flags deficiencies instantly
  • ACORD form recognition: Specialized handling for ACORD 25, ACORD 28, and other standard insurance certificate formats

Fleet & DOT Compliance

For trucking companies, delivery services, and businesses with commercial vehicle fleets:

  • Driver licenses (CDL): Extract license number, class, endorsements (Hazmat, Tanker, Doubles/Triples), restrictions, and expiration dates
  • Medical certificates: Capture DOT medical card details including examiner information, issue date, and expiration
  • Vehicle registrations: Parse registration documents for VIN, plate number, registration expiration, and registered weight
  • Inspection reports: Extract key findings from DOT inspection reports and annual vehicle inspection certificates
  • Drug test results: Capture test dates, results, and compliance status from consortium documentation

Lease & Contract Management

For multi-location retailers, franchise operators, and commercial real estate managers:

  • Commercial leases: Extract base rent, CAM charges, rent escalation clauses, lease term, renewal options, and notice periods
  • Lease abstracts: Automatically generate summary sheets from full lease documents with key dates and financial terms
  • Amendment tracking: Parse lease amendments and modifications, linking them to the original agreement
  • Option deadlines: Identify renewal option windows, termination rights, and expansion/contraction clauses with associated dates
  • Landlord/tenant obligations: Extract maintenance responsibilities, insurance requirements, and permitted use clauses

Workers' Compensation & HR

For staffing agencies, construction firms, and businesses with large hourly workforces:

  • Workers' comp policies: Extract experience modification rate (EMR/MOD), class codes, payroll estimates, and premium amounts
  • Payroll reports: Parse quarterly payroll summaries by classification code for audit preparation
  • Employee certifications: Capture training certificates, safety certifications (OSHA 10/30, forklift), and expiration dates
  • I-9 documents: Extract relevant data from identity and work authorization documents for compliance verification

Utility & Facility Management

For facility managers, property portfolios, and multi-site businesses:

  • Utility bills: Extract account numbers, meter readings, usage (kWh, therms, gallons), demand charges, rate schedules, and total amounts
  • Permit documents: Parse permit numbers, issue dates, expiration dates, inspection requirements, and scope of work
  • Inspection reports: Capture findings from fire marshal inspections, elevator certificates, and health department reviews
  • Equipment certifications: Extract warranty information, maintenance schedules, and compliance certificates for HVAC, fire systems, and elevators

Accuracy & Performance

  • 95%+ extraction accuracy: Our AI models are trained on thousands of real compliance documents across industries
  • Processing time: Most documents processed in under 30 seconds - even complex multi-page leases
  • Continuous improvement: The system learns from corrections, improving accuracy for your specific document types over time
  • Audit trail: Every extraction is logged with the original document, extracted values, confidence scores, and any manual edits
