Smart Alerts & Notifications

Never miss a deadline again. Automated reminders sent 60, 30, 14, and 7 days before expirations with escalation workflows that ensure action is taken.

Configurable Alert Schedules

  • Multi-stage reminders: Set up cascading alerts at 60, 30, 14, 7, and 1 day before any deadline - customize intervals for each requirement type
  • Overdue escalation: When deadlines pass, alerts escalate to managers and executives on a configurable schedule
  • Quiet hours: Configure when alerts are sent to avoid after-hours notifications - batch morning digests for non-urgent items
  • Per-item customization: Override default schedules for high-priority items that need more lead time or more frequent reminders

Insurance & COI Alerts

For risk managers, property managers, and procurement teams:

  • COI expiration warnings: Automatic alerts when vendor certificates are approaching expiration - sent to your team and directly to vendors
  • Coverage gap notifications: Instant alerts when a new COI doesn't meet your minimum coverage requirements
  • Vendor non-response escalation: If a vendor doesn't respond to renewal requests, escalate to project managers who can halt work if needed
  • Policy cancellation alerts: Integration with insurance verification services to notify you immediately if a vendor's policy is cancelled mid-term
  • Bulk renewal campaigns: Automated email campaigns to all vendors with expiring COIs, with tracking to see who has responded

Fleet & Driver Compliance Alerts

For fleet managers, safety directors, and dispatch coordinators:

  • CDL expiration alerts: Notify drivers and supervisors well in advance of license expirations - prevent drivers from operating with expired credentials
  • Medical card reminders: DOT medical certificates expire frequently - alerts ensure drivers stay qualified without last-minute scrambles
  • Vehicle registration warnings: Alerts for upcoming registration renewals with enough lead time to complete paperwork
  • Annual inspection due dates: Track when vehicles need their next DOT annual inspection and alert maintenance teams
  • Drug test scheduling: Reminders for random pool testing, post-accident tests, and return-to-duty requirements
  • Hours of Service warnings: Integration with ELD systems to alert when drivers are approaching HOS limits

Lease & Contract Deadline Alerts

For real estate managers, franchise operators, and legal teams:

  • Renewal option deadlines: Critical alerts 180, 90, 60, and 30 days before renewal option exercise dates - missing these can cost thousands
  • Termination notice windows: Alerts when you're entering the window to provide termination notice without penalty
  • Rent escalation dates: Reminders when rent increases take effect so you can update budgets and verify landlord calculations
  • CAM reconciliation deadlines: Alerts to request and review annual CAM reconciliation statements
  • Insurance delivery requirements: Many leases require annual proof of insurance - alerts ensure you don't breach
  • Auto-renewal warnings: Alert before contracts auto-renew so you have time to renegotiate or cancel

Permit & License Renewal Alerts

For facility managers, restaurant operators, and multi-location businesses:

  • Business license renewals: Alerts before city, county, and state business licenses expire - avoid fines and operating restrictions
  • Health permit expirations: Critical for restaurants and food service - alerts ensure inspections are scheduled in time
  • Fire inspection scheduling: Reminders to schedule required annual fire marshal inspections
  • Elevator and equipment certifications: Track certification expirations for elevators, boilers, and pressure vessels
  • Liquor license renewals: High-stakes deadlines that can shut down operations if missed
  • Professional license tracking: For businesses employing licensed professionals (engineers, accountants, nurses)

Workers' Comp & HR Alerts

For HR managers, safety coordinators, and payroll teams:

  • Policy renewal reminders: Alerts 90 and 60 days before workers' comp policy renewal with reminders to gather payroll data
  • Audit preparation alerts: When your audit window approaches, alerts prompt you to compile required documentation
  • Employee certification expirations: Track OSHA training, forklift certifications, and other required credentials
  • I-9 reverification dates: Alerts when work authorization documents expire and reverification is required
  • Benefits enrollment windows: Reminders for open enrollment periods and new hire enrollment deadlines

Delivery Channels

  • Email notifications: Rich HTML emails with action buttons - view details, mark complete, or snooze
  • Daily digest: Consolidated morning email summarizing all upcoming deadlines and overdue items
  • In-app notifications: Bell icon with badge count shows pending alerts when you log in
  • SMS text alerts: Optional text messages for critical deadlines (configurable by urgency level)
  • Calendar integration: Sync deadlines to Google Calendar, Outlook, or iCal with automatic updates
  • Slack/Teams integration: Post alerts to dedicated compliance channels for team visibility

Escalation Workflows

  • Role-based routing: Alerts go to the right person based on entity type, location, or department
  • Escalation paths: Define who gets notified if the primary owner doesn't act within a specified time
  • Manager overrides: Managers can reassign or acknowledge items on behalf of their team
  • Vendor notifications: Automatically send renewal requests directly to vendors with secure upload links
  • Acknowledgment tracking: See who has viewed alerts and whether action has been taken
