Real-Time Compliance Dashboard

See your entire compliance status at a glance. Track what's current, expiring soon, and overdue across all your vendors, properties, vehicles, and documents.

Compliance Overview

  • Traffic light status: See at-a-glance how many items are compliant (green), expiring soon (yellow), or overdue (red)
  • Compliance score: Single percentage that represents your overall compliance health - track trends over time
  • Category breakdown: View compliance status by type - insurance, licenses, certifications, contracts, inspections
  • Location/department filters: Drill down by location, department, or business unit to see localized compliance status
  • Trend charts: Visualize how your compliance posture has improved (or declined) over weeks and months

Vendor & COI Dashboard

For property managers, GCs, and procurement teams managing vendor compliance:

  • Vendor compliance grid: See all vendors with their current COI status - compliant, expiring within 30/60/90 days, or expired
  • Coverage summary: Quick view of GL, Auto, Umbrella, and Workers' Comp coverage across all vendors
  • Non-compliant vendor list: Prioritized list of vendors who need attention, sorted by urgency and risk level
  • Vendor scorecards: Individual vendor pages showing complete compliance history, all documents, and communication log
  • Project-based views: Filter vendors by project or job site to see compliance status for specific work
  • Audit-ready reports: One-click export of all vendor COIs and compliance status for insurance audits or client requests

Fleet Compliance Dashboard

For fleet managers, safety directors, and transportation companies:

  • Driver qualification status: Real-time view of every driver's CDL, medical card, MVR, and drug test status
  • Vehicle compliance grid: All vehicles with registration, inspection, and maintenance compliance status
  • DOT audit readiness score: Composite metric showing how prepared you are for a DOT compliance review
  • Out-of-service risk: Flagged drivers or vehicles that could be put out of service if inspected today
  • Expiration calendar: Visual calendar showing all upcoming driver and vehicle expirations
  • Driver files: Individual driver pages with complete DQ file contents and document history

Property & Lease Dashboard

For real estate managers, multi-location retailers, and franchise operators:

  • Portfolio overview: All properties with lease status, key dates, and compliance requirements
  • Critical date timeline: Visual timeline of upcoming renewals, expirations, and option deadlines across portfolio
  • Rent roll summary: Current rent obligations with upcoming escalations highlighted
  • Lease obligation tracker: Status of landlord and tenant obligations - insurance deliveries, maintenance, etc.
  • Property scorecards: Individual location pages with all lease terms, documents, and compliance items
  • Financial impact view: See the dollar impact of upcoming rent changes, CAM estimates, and renewals

Permit & License Dashboard

For facility managers, restaurant operators, and compliance officers:

  • License inventory: Complete list of all business licenses, permits, and certifications with status
  • Jurisdiction breakdown: View compliance status by city, county, state, or regulatory body
  • Inspection calendar: Upcoming and overdue inspections for fire, health, building, and other agencies
  • Location compliance matrix: Grid view showing which licenses/permits each location has (and is missing)
  • Renewal pipeline: All items coming due in the next 30/60/90 days with status and owner assignment
  • Cost tracking: Annual spend on licenses and permits by location and category

HR & Employee Compliance Dashboard

For HR managers, staffing agencies, and safety coordinators:

  • Employee certification tracker: View all employees with their required certifications and expiration status
  • Training compliance: Track required training completion (OSHA, harassment prevention, etc.) by employee and department
  • Work authorization status: I-9 compliance with reverification dates flagged
  • Department compliance scores: Compare compliance rates across departments to identify problem areas
  • New hire checklist: Track onboarding compliance tasks for new employees
  • Audit preparation: Ready access to all employee compliance documentation for audits

Document Repository

  • Centralized storage: All compliance documents in one searchable repository - no more hunting through email and file shares
  • Smart search: Find documents by vendor name, policy number, date range, document type, or extracted content
  • Version history: Track document versions over time - see previous COIs, old lease amendments, etc.
  • Document preview: View PDFs and images directly in the browser without downloading
  • Bulk download: Export all documents for a vendor, location, or time period as a ZIP file
  • Retention policies: Automatic archival based on configurable retention rules

Reporting & Exports

  • Scheduled reports: Automatic weekly or monthly compliance summaries delivered via email
  • Custom report builder: Create reports filtered by entity type, status, date range, location, and more
  • Export formats: Download reports as PDF, Excel, or CSV for sharing with stakeholders
  • Board/executive summaries: High-level compliance scorecards designed for leadership review
  • Audit packages: Pre-formatted document bundles for insurance audits, due diligence, or regulatory reviews
  • API access: Pull compliance data into your BI tools or other systems via REST API

User Experience

  • Role-based views: Different dashboard layouts for executives, managers, and front-line staff
  • Customizable widgets: Arrange and configure dashboard widgets to show what matters most to you
  • Mobile responsive: Full dashboard functionality on tablets and phones for on-the-go access
  • Quick actions: Upload documents, mark items complete, or send reminders without leaving the dashboard
  • Saved filters: Save frequently used filter combinations for one-click access
  • White-label ready: Dashboard can be branded with your company logo and colors
